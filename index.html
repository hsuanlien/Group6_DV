Observations

Elevation notes

  • High-elevation airports are geographically concentrated rather than globally distributed.
  • Airport connectivity is not proportional to elevation.
  • Aircraft usage becomes less diverse at higher elevations.
  • Only a few aircraft types dominate operations at high-elevation airports.
  • Elevation influences aircraft choice more than route volume.

Hubs notes

  • Which airports dominate departures?
  • Do hubs differ by region?

Distance notes

  • Compare short-haul vs long-haul clusters.
  • Look for outliers (very long routes).

Curvature notes

  • Great-circle paths bend at higher latitudes.
  • Polar-like routing may appear curved on the map.
